Shrimp & Orzo Primavera

Author Recipe Bridge

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 400 g large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 360 g dry orzo pasta
  • 300 g zucchini, halved and sliced into half-moons
  • 200 g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 150 g sweet peas (frozen, thawed)
  • 25 ml olive oil
  • 30 g pine nuts, toasted (optional)
  • 30 g Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
  • 15 ml fresh lemon juice
  • 2 g garlic powder
  • 2 g salt
  • 1 g black pepper
  • Zest of 1 lemon (optional)

Instructions

  • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook orzo according to package directions (≈8-10 min) until al dente; drain and set aside.
  • While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add shrimp; season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Cook 2-3 min per side until pink and opaque; remove and set aside.
  • In the same skillet, add zucchini; sauté 3-4 min until just tender. Add cherry tomatoes and peas; cook another 2 min.
  • Return shrimp to the pan; add cooked orzo, lemon juice, lemon zest (if using), and toss to combine.
  • Stir in toasted pine nuts and grated Parmesan; heat through for 1 min.
  • Divide into 4 bowls and serve warm.
Servings 4
