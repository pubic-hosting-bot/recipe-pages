Mediterranean Turkey & Orzo Skillet

Approx. 600 kcal per serving.
Author Recipe Bridge

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg ground turkey (93 % lean)
  • 2 cups pearl orzo (dry)
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • 1 l arge cucumber (diced)
  • 1 red onion (thinly sliced)
  • ½ cup Kalamata olives (pitted & halved)
  • ¼ cup feta cheese (crumbled, ~30 g per serving)
  • 3 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • salt, pepper
  • fresh parsley (optional)

Instructions

  • Cook orzo in salted boiling water al dente (≈ 9 min); drain.
  • Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add ground turkey, breaking it up; cook until no longer pink (≈ 8 min).
  • Stir in oregano, garlic powder, salt and pepper.
  • Add cherry tomatoes, red onion, and olives; sauté 3-4 min until tomatoes soften.
  • Mix cooked orzo into the skillet; heat through 2 min.
  • Remove from heat, fold in cucumber, crumbled feta, and chopped parsley.
  • Divide into 6 containers; garnish with extra parsley or a lemon wedge if desired.
Servings 6
