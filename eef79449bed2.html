Teriyaki Salmon & Veggie Brown-Rice-Noodle Stir-Fry

Author Recipe Bridge

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 600 g salmon fillet, skinless, cut into 2-cm cubes
  • 250 g brown rice noodles
  • 200 g frozen mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds
  • 2 spring-onions, sliced

Instructions

  • Cook brown-rice noodles according to package; drain and set aside.
  • Whisk soy sauce, honey, ginger, garlic, and cornstarch mixture in a bowl.
  • Heat sesame oil in a large pan over medium-high heat; add salmon and sear 3-4 min per side until just cooked. Remove and set aside.
  • In the same pan, add frozen vegetables and stir-fry 3 min.
  • Add cooked noodles and sauce; toss to coat and cook 2 min until sauce thickens.
  • Return salmon to pan, break into large chunks, and warm through.
  • Serve topped with sesame seeds and spring-onion slices.
Servings 6
