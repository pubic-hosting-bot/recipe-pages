Chicken & Veggie Stir-Fry

~580 kcal per serving. Lactose-friendly; optional sesame seeds.
Author Recipe Bridge

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 600 g skinless chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) sesame oil
  • 200 g broccoli florets
  • 150 g red bell pepper, sliced
  • 150 g carrots, julienned
  • 100 g snow peas
  • 2 tbsp (30 g) minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp (30 g) fresh ginger, grated
  • 60 ml low-sodium soy sauce
  • 30 ml rice vinegar
  • 15 ml honey
  • 1 tsp (5 g) cornstarch mixed with 30 ml water
  • 1 tsp (5 g) toasted sesame seeds (optional)
  • Pinch of black pepper

Instructions

  • Marinate chicken - Toss the sliced chicken with 1 tbsp sesame oil, 30 ml soy sauce, and black pepper. Let sit 5 min.
  • Heat wok - Add the remaining sesame oil over high heat.
  • Cook aromatics - Stir-fry garlic and ginger for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  • Sear chicken - Add chicken and stir-fry 4-5 min, until just cooked through. Transfer to a plate.
  • Vegetables - Add broccoli, bell pepper, carrots, and snow peas. Stir-fry 3-4 min, keeping crisp-tender.
  • Sauce - Mix rice vinegar and honey; pour over veggies. Add cornstarch slurry, stirring until sauce thickens (≈1 min).
  • Combine - Return chicken to wok, toss to coat evenly. Heat 1 min more.
  • Finish - Sprinkle sesame seeds if using; serve hot over steamed rice or cauliflower rice.
Servings 4
