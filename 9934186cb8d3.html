Egg-White & Veggie Frittata

Author Recipe Bridge

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 200 g egg whites
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 100 g spinach, chopped
  • 80 g bell pepper, diced
  • 80 g zucchini, grated
  • 30 g onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (15 ml)
  • 30 g low-fat cheese, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 180°C.
  • In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion and bell pepper, sauté 3-4 minutes.
  • Add zucchini and spinach, cook until wilted, about 2 minutes.
  • In a bowl, whisk egg whites, whole eggs, salt, and pepper.
  • Pour the egg mixture over the vegetables in the skillet, stirring gently to combine.
  • Sprinkle grated cheese on top.
  • Transfer skillet to oven and bake 12-15 minutes until set and lightly golden.
  • Remove, let cool slightly, slice, and serve.
Servings 4
