Mexican-Style Turkey-Black-Bean Burrito Bowl

Author Recipe Bridge

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 500 g ground turkey
  • 200 g black beans, cooked and drained
  • 150 g brown rice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small red bell pepper, diced (≈70 g)
  • 1 small onion, diced (≈50 g)
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • ½ tsp smoked paprika
  • 120 g low-fat shredded cheese
  • ½ avocado, sliced
  • 2 tbsp salsa
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  • Cook brown rice according to package; set aside.
  • Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add onion and bell pepper; sauté 4 min.
  • Add ground turkey, breaking it up; cook until browned, about 6 min.
  • Stir in chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper; cook 1 min.
  • Add black beans and heat through 2 min.
  • Divide rice into 6 bowls, top with turkey-bean mixture, cheese, avocado, salsa, and cilantro.
Servings 6
