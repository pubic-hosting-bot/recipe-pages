Teriyaki Salmon & Veggie Brown-Rice-Noodle Stir-Fry

Each serving ≈ 610 kcal.
Author Recipe Bridge

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 200 g salmon fillets (≈ 12 × 100 g pieces)
  • 1 200 g brown-rice-noodles (dry)
  • 600 g broccoli florets
  • 300 g carrot, thin matchsticks
  • 90 ml low-sodium teriyaki sauce
  • 30 ml sesame oil
  • 30 ml olive oil
  • 15 g white sesame seeds (optional)
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  • Cook brown-rice-noodles per package, drain.
  • Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Season salmon, sear 3-4 min per side, remove and slice.
  • In same pan add sesame oil, broccoli and carrot; stir-fry 4-5 min until crisp-tender.
  • Add noodles and teriyaki sauce, toss 1-2 min.
  • Return salmon to pan, drizzle extra sauce if desired, sprinkle sesame seeds.
  • Serve hot.
Servings 6
