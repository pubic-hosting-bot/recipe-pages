Greek-Style Turkey & Veggie Skillet

Author Recipe Bridge

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 500 g ground turkey (lean ≤7 % fat)
  • 300 g zucchini, sliced
  • 200 g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 80 g Kalamata olives, sliced
  • 120 g red onion, diced
  • 6 g garlic, minced (≈2 cloves)
  • 30 ml extra-virgin olive oil
  • 15 ml red-wine vinegar
  • 5 g dried oregano
  • 3 g smoked paprika
  • 1 g black pepper
  • 1 g salt
  • 150 g cooked whole-grain couscous (optional carb base)
  • 20 g feta cheese, crumbled (optional, ≤5 g per serving)

Instructions

  • Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  • Add the diced onion and garlic; sauté 2-3 min until fragrant.
  • Add the ground turkey, breaking it up with a spatula; cook 5-6 min until browned.
  • Stir in the sliced zucchini, cherry tomatoes, and olives; cook 4-5 min until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  • Sprinkle dried oregano, smoked paprika, black pepper, and salt; mix well.
  • Drizzle the red-wine vinegar, stir, and let the mixture simmer 2 min.
  • If using couscous, fluff it onto plates and top with the turkey-veggie mixture.
  • Finish with a light sprinkle of feta (optional) and a pinch of fresh oregano, then serve.
Servings 4
