Teriyaki Glazed Salmon with Brown Rice & Broccoli

Author Recipe Bridge

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 4 × 150 g salmon fillets (600 g total)
  • 200 g brown rice (uncooked)
  • 300 g broccoli florets
  • 15 ml low-sodium soy sauce
  • 15 ml mirin
  • 10 ml rice vinegar
  • 10 g honey
  • 5 g grated ginger
  • 2 g garlic powder
  • 5 ml sesame oil
  • 5 ml olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  • Rinse rice; cook 200 g brown rice with 400 ml water. Bring to boil, reduce heat, cover 40 min.
  • Preheat oven to 200 °C. Line baking tray.
  • Mix soy sauce, mirin, rice vinegar, honey, ginger, garlic powder, sesame oil.
  • Place salmon fillets on tray, season with salt & pepper, brush with teriyaki mixture.
  • Roast salmon 12-15 min until flaky.
  • Steam broccoli 5-6 min until bright green.
  • Portion: 100 g cooked rice, 150 g salmon, 100 g broccoli per serving. Drizzle extra teriyaki sauce if desired.
Servings 6
