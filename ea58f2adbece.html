Spicy Chicken & Veggie Stir-fry

Author Recipe Bridge

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 400 g chicken breast
  • 300 g red bell pepper
  • 300 g yellow bell pepper
  • 250 g broccoli florets
  • 120 g carrot
  • 200 g cooked brown rice
  • 30 ml low-sodium soy sauce
  • 10 ml sriracha
  • 10 g fresh ginger, minced
  • 2 g garlic powder
  • 5 ml sesame oil
  • 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 20 ml water

Instructions

  • Heat sesame oil in a wok over medium-high heat.
  • Add ginger and garlic powder, stir 30 s.
  • Add chicken strips, cook until browned (≈ 5 min).
  • Add sliced bell peppers and carrot; stir-fry 3 min.
  • Add broccoli and cooked brown rice; stir 2 min.
  • Mix soy sauce, sriracha, and cornstarch slurry; pour over skillet, toss until sauce thickens.
  • Serve hot.
Servings 4
