Baked Herb-Garlic Salmon with Roasted Veggies & Couscous

Approx. 600 kcal per serving. Adjust olive oil or feta to fine-tune calories. No lemon, barley, lentils, quinoa, brown rice, or chili.
Author Recipe Bridge

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 900 g salmon fillets, skin-on
  • 30 ml olive oil
  • 6 g garlic, minced
  • 4 g dried oregano
  • 2 g dried thyme
  • 6 g smoked paprika
  • 3 g salt
  • 1.5 g black pepper
  • 600 g sweet potato, peeled & 1-cm dice
  • 300 g broccoli florets
  • 200 g carrot, peeled & sliced into half-coins
  • 300 g couscous (dry)
  • 120 g feta cheese, crumbled (optional)
  • 12 g fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions

  • Pre-heat the oven to 200 °C (400 °F). Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.
  • Toss sweet potato, broccoli, and carrot with 15 ml olive oil, half the garlic, half the oregano, thyme, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper; spread evenly on the sheet.
  • Pat salmon dry, drizzle with remaining 15 ml olive oil, then rub with remaining garlic, oregano, thyme, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Place skin-side down on the sheet.
  • Bake 25-30 minutes, stirring vegetables halfway, until salmon reaches ~62 °C (145 °F) and vegetables are tender-caramelized.
  • While baking, place dry couscous in a bowl, pour 300 ml boiling water over it, cover, let sit 5 minutes, then fluff with a fork; drizzle with a tiny splash of olive oil if desired and season lightly with salt.
  • To serve, divide couscous among 6 bowls, top each with a salmon fillet, roasted vegetables, and optional feta and parsley.
Servings 6
