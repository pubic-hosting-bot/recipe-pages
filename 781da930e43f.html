Teriyaki Salmon & Veggie Brown-Rice-Noodle Stir-Fry

Author Recipe Bridge

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 500 g salmon fillets
  • 200 g rice noodles (dry)
  • 300 g broccoli florets
  • 300 g carrot (matchsticks)
  • 90 ml low-sodium teriyaki sauce
  • 30 ml sesame oil
  • 30 ml olive oil
  • 15 g white sesame seeds (optional)
  • salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  • Cook rice-noodles according to package, drain.
  • Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Season salmon with salt & pepper, sear 3-4 min per side until cooked through; remove and slice.
  • In same pan add sesame oil, broccoli and carrot; stir-fry 4-5 min until crisp-tender.
  • Add noodles and teriyaki sauce, toss 1-2 min.
  • Return salmon to pan, drizzle extra sauce if desired, sprinkle sesame seeds.
  • Serve hot.
Servings 6
