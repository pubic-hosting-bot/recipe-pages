Mediterranean Chickpea-Spinach Rice Bowl

Author Recipe Bridge

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 200 g brown rice
  • 400 g canned chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 150 g fresh spinach
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small red onion, finely diced (≈50 g)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 30 g feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  • Cook brown rice according to package directions; set aside.
  • Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Sauté onion until translucent, about 3 min.
  • Add garlic, smoked paprika, and oregano; cook 30 s.
  • Stir in chickpeas and cook 3-4 min until heated through.
  • Add spinach and cook until wilted, about 2 min.
  • Season with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
  • Divide rice among 6 bowls, top with the chickpea-spinach mixture, and sprinkle feta.
Servings 6
