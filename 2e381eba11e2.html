Mediterranean Chickpea-Spinach Rice Bowl

Approximately 590 kcal per serving.
Author Recipe Bridge

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 480 g cooked brown rice*
  • 960 g canned chickpeas (drained, rinsed)
  • 300 g baby spinach
  • 150 g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 65 g red onion, thinly sliced
  • 90 ml extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 30 ml fresh lemon juice
  • 30 g feta cheese, crumbled (optional)
  • salt & freshly ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  • Cook brown rice according to package instructions.
  • Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and onion, sauté 2-3 min.
  • Add chickpeas and cherry tomatoes, cook 3-4 min.
  • Stir in spinach until wilted.
  • Toss cooked rice with lemon juice, season with salt & pepper.
  • Divide rice into bowls, top with chickpea-veggie mix and sprinkle feta.
Servings 6
