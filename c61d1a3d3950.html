Mexican-Style Turkey-Black-Bean Burrito Bowl

Author Recipe Bridge

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 500 g turkey mince
  • 300 g dry brown rice (≈ 720 g cooked)
  • 400 g canned black beans (drained)
  • 400 g corn kernels
  • 1 avocado
  • juice of 1 lime
  • 30 g shredded cheese (optional, ≤ 1 Tbsp per serving)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 5 g chili powder
  • 250 g cherry tomatoes
  • salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  • Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add ground turkey, season with salt, pepper, and chili powder; cook 6-8 min until browned.
  • While turkey cooks, microwave rice if not already hot.
  • Combine cooked turkey, black beans, corn, and rice in a bowl.
  • Add avocado, cherry tomatoes, lime juice, and cheese (if using).
  • Mix gently and serve hot or at room temperature.
Servings 4
