Lemon Herb Chickpea & Couscous Bowl

Approx. 600 kcal per serving. Adjust feta or olive oil to fine-tune calories. No brown rice, no chili, no lentils, no quinoa.
Author Recipe Bridge

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 420 g couscous (dry)
  • 780 g canned chickpeas, drained & rinsed
  • 300 g zucchini, diced
  • 300 g red bell pepper, diced
  • 300 g carrot, diced
  • 72 ml olive oil
  • 15 ml lemon juice
  • 36 g garlic, minced (≈6 cloves)
  • 12 g dried oregano
  • 6 g salt
  • 3 g black pepper
  • 90 g feta cheese, crumbled (optional)

Instructions

  • Place dry couscous in a large bowl, pour 630 ml boiling water over it, cover, let sit 5 min, then fluff with fork.
  • While couscous rests, preheat oven to 200 °C (400 °F). Toss zucchini, bell pepper, and carrot with 18 ml olive oil, salt, pepper, and half the lemon juice; spread on a baking sheet.
  • Roast vegetables 20-25 min, stirring halfway, until tender and lightly caramelized.
  • In a skillet, heat remaining 18 ml olive oil over medium-high heat. Add garlic and oregano, sauté 30 s until fragrant.
  • Add chickpeas, season with salt and pepper, cook 4-5 min until warmed through and slightly crisped.
  • In a large bowl combine cooked couscous, roasted vegetables, and chickpea mixture. Drizzle with remaining lemon juice and olive oil; toss to combine.
  • Divide into 6 bowls, top each with 15 g feta cheese (if using), and serve warm or cold.
Servings 6
