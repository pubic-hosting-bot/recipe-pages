Mediterranean Chickpea-Spinach Rice Bowl

Use low-fat feta to keep calories around 590 kcal per serving.
Author Recipe Bridge

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 300 g brown rice (dry)
  • 400 g canned chickpeas, drained
  • 200 g fresh spinach
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 150 g feta cheese (optional, ≤ 10 g per serving)
  • 50 g kalamata olives, sliced
  • 1 l emon, juice
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  • Cook brown rice according to package, then fluff.
  • Heat olive oil in a pan, add smoked paprika and cumin, stir 30 s.
  • Add chickpeas, cook 3-4 min until warmed.
  • Add spinach, cook until wilted.
  • Stir in cooked rice, lemon juice, olives, and season.
  • Serve topped with crumbled feta (if using).
Servings 6
