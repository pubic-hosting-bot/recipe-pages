Lemon-Herb Grilled Chicken Bowl

Author Recipe Bridge

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts (≈ 600 g)
  • 30 ml olive oil
  • 15 ml lemon juice
  • 2 g dried oregano
  • 2 g dried thyme
  • 2 g garlic powder
  • 200 g brown rice
  • 300 g mixed vegetables (broccoli, carrots, bell pepper)
  • 10 g salt
  • 2 g black pepper

Instructions

  • Preheat grill/pan to medium-high.
  • Mix oil, lemon juice, herbs, garlic powder, salt, pepper.
  • Coat chicken, grill 6-7 min each side until internal 75 °C.
  • Cook brown rice (2 × water) 20 min.
  • Steam or stir-fry vegetables 5-6 min.
  • Slice chicken, assemble bowls with rice, vegetables, chicken.
Servings 4
