Lemon Herb Baked Chicken Thighs with Roasted Veggies & Couscous

Approx. 610 kcal per serving. Adjust almonds or olive oil to fine-tune calories.
Author Recipe Bridge

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 600 g chicken thighs, boneless, skinless
  • 200 g couscous (dry)
  • 200 g zucchini, diced
  • 200 g red bell pepper, diced
  • 200 g carrot, diced
  • 60 ml olive oil
  • 2 l emons (zest and juice)
  • 4 g garlic, minced (≈4 cloves)
  • 4 g dried oregano
  • 2 g salt
  • 1 g black pepper
  • 80 g feta cheese, crumbled
  • 60 g sliced almonds

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 200 °C (400 °F).
  • Toss zucchini, bell pepper, and carrot with 15 ml olive oil, salt, pepper, and half the lemon zest; spread on a baking sheet.
  • Roast vegetables 20-25 min, stirring halfway, until tender and lightly caramelized.
  • Meanwhile, season chicken thighs with remaining lemon zest, juice, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper; drizzle with 15 ml olive oil.
  • Place chicken on a second baking sheet; bake 25-30 min until internal temperature reaches 75 °C (165 °F).
  • While chicken and veggies cook, prepare couscous: place dry couscous in a bowl, pour 200 ml boiling water over it, cover, let sit 5 min, then fluff with fork; drizzle with remaining 15 ml olive oil and season with salt.
  • To serve, divide couscous among 4 plates, top with roasted vegetables, a chicken thigh, sprinkle with feta and sliced almonds, and drizzle any pan juices.
Servings 4
