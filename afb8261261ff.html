Turkey & Black Bean Chili

Author Recipe Bridge

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 600 g lean ground turkey (≤7% fat)
  • 800 g canned black beans, drained and rinsed (≈2 × 400 g cans)
  • 400 g canned diced tomatoes
  • 150 g sweet corn kernels (frozen, thawed)
  • 150 g onion, chopped
  • 150 g red bell pepper, diced
  • 20 ml olive oil
  • 15 g tomato paste
  • 4 g chili powder
  • 2 g ground cumin
  • 1 g smoked paprika
  • 1 g garlic powder
  • 1 g salt
  • 0.5 g black pepper
  • 120 g plain Greek yogurt (optional topping, 30 g per serving)

Instructions

  • Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and bell pepper; sauté 4-5 min until softened.
  • Add ground turkey; cook, breaking it up, until browned (≈6-8 min).
  • Stir in tomato paste, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper; cook 1 min.
  • Add black beans, corn, and diced tomatoes (with their juice). Stir to combine.
  • Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer 20-25 min, stirring occasionally, until thickened.
  • Taste and adjust seasoning. Serve topped with a dollop of Greek yogurt if desired.
Servings 4
