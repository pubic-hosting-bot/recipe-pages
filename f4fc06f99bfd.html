Spicy Chicken Burrito Bowls

Around 600 kcal per serving.
Author Recipe Bridge

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 2 lb chicken breast (diced)
  • 1 cup brown rice (uncooked)
  • 1 can black beans (drained)
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 red bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 avocado (sliced)
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • ½ tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • salt, pepper
  • ¼ cup shredded cheddar (~30 g per serving)
  • lime wedges
  • fresh cilantro

Instructions

  • Cook brown rice according to package (≈ 20 min).
  • In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat; add chicken and season with chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt & pepper. Cook until golden (≈ 8 min).
  • Add bell pepper, corn, and black beans; sauté 3-4 min.
  • Assemble bowls: layer rice, chicken mixture, avocado, shredded cheddar, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime.
Servings 6
