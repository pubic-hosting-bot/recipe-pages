Sweet Potato & Chicken Skillet

Author Recipe Bridge

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 800 g chicken breast, skinless, diced
  • 1000 g sweet potato, peeled & cubed (≈1-cm cubes)
  • 150 g red bell pepper, diced
  • 120 g onion, chopped
  • 100 g kale, stems removed, chopped
  • 30 ml olive oil
  • 2 g smoked paprika
  • 2 g ground cumin
  • 1 g garlic powder
  • 1 g salt
  • 0.5 g black pepper
  • 80 g feta cheese, crumbled (optional)
  • 15 ml lemon juice (optional)

Instructions

  • Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and bell pepper; sauté 3-4 min until softened.
  • Add chicken dice; cook 6-8 min, stirring, until browned and cooked through.
  • Stir in sweet potato cubes; cook 5-6 min, stirring occasionally, until they start to soften.
  • Sprinkle smoked paprika, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper; stir to coat.
  • Add kale; cook 2-3 min until wilted.
  • Drizzle lemon juice, sprinkle feta (if using), and give a final toss.
  • Serve hot, divided into 4 equal portions.
Servings 4
